Open hybrid multicloud

Supporting 30+ providers, this elastically scaling fabric provider numerous advantages, including:

  • Reduced risk (no [closed] vendor lock-in), and redundancy
  • Geo-redundancy across multiple providers (e.g.: store all finance/health data in country of origin)
  • Buying power (get better deals from providers)

Big data

  • Apache's Hadoop, Spark, Mesos and Zeppelin support
  • Extract Transform Load (ETL) processes for staging your data
  • Experiment—ad-hoc querying—interfaces
  • Scheduled jobs (e.g.: update global recommendations at 3AM)
